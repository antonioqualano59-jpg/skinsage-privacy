SkinSage Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SkinSage ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Email address and password (via Firebase Authentication), or Google account info if you sign in with Google.
  • Profile data: Name, age, skin type, and skin concerns you provide.
  • Usage data: Daily routine logs, skin feeling ratings, weekly check-ins, habit scores, and product logs.
  • Photos: Skin progress photos you choose to upload, stored securely in Firebase Storage.
  • AI conversations: Messages you send to the Skin AI feature. These are processed by Google Gemini and are not stored permanently.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized skincare routines, tips, and AI advice based on your skin type and logged data.
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase (Google Cloud).
  • To improve your in-app experience — streak tracking, habit scores, and progress comparisons.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties. Ever.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini: Powers the Skin AI chat feature. Messages are sent to Google's API for processing. Google Privacy Policy
  • Open Beauty Facts: Used for barcode ingredient lookups. No personal data is shared.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase servers with industry-standard encryption. Access is restricted to your authenticated account only — no other user can access your data.

5. Photos

Skin progress photos are stored in Firebase Storage and are only accessible by you. Photo analysis is performed entirely on-device using Apple's Vision framework — photos are never sent to external servers for analysis.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

SkinSage is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
